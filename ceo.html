About:Erick Amollo – Founder, Charming Afrika Erick Amollo is a passionate tourism enthusiast and changemaker dedicated to shaping a more sustainable and inclusive future for African tourism. He founded Charming Afrika as a platform that educates, inspires, and connects young Africans through responsible travel, cultural awareness, and humanitarian action. Under his leadership, Charming Afrika continues to engage with tourism stakeholders, young policy makers, and youth in tourism through insightful interviews, storytelling, and collaborative initiatives that highlight the importance of eco-tourism and community-driven development. Erick’s vision is to see Charming Afrika grow into a continental movement that not only showcases Africa’s hidden gems but also empowers a new generation of conscious travelers and leaders committed to protecting the continent’s natural and cultural heritage.
